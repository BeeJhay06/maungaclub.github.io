About Pukeko Chalet

Welcome to the tranquil Pukeko Chalet at the Maunga Club, a lakeside paradise where relaxation takes center stage. Nestled by the water's edge, this chalet offers a serene retreat, where the soothing sounds of nature and breathtaking views create an atmosphere of serenity.

The adult bedroom in Pukeko Chalet is designed with your comfort in mind. Its cozy ambiance and carefully curated furnishings provide the perfect setting for unwinding after a day of exploration. With views of the lake and the surrounding landscape, you'll wake up feeling refreshed and rejuvenated.

The bathroom is a sanctuary of luxury, adorned with modern amenities and elegant design. Pamper yourself with a hot bath or a refreshing shower, surrounded by nature's beauty through large windows that frame the stunning outdoors.

As you step outside onto the chalet's exterior, you'll be greeted by the picturesque view of the lake and the tranquil surroundings. Whether you're enjoying a leisurely morning coffee or a quiet evening under the stars, the lakeside setting offers the perfect backdrop for creating unforgettable memories.

Experience the essence of relaxation and escape at Pukeko Chalet, where the combination of natural beauty and comfortable living spaces invite you to immerse yourself in the magic of lakeside living.
