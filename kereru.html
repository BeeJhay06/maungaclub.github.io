About Kereru Chalet

Welcome to the serene and luxurious Kereru Chalet at the Maunga Club. Tucked away in the heart of nature, this chalet offers an escape from the hustle and bustle of everyday life. Whether you're seeking a peaceful retreat or an adventure-filled getaway, Kereru Chalet has something to offer for everyone.

Featuring spacious and beautifully designed rooms, the chalet provides an unparalleled level of comfort. The elegantly furnished adult bedroom offers a cozy haven for relaxation, while the vibrant and playful kids' bedroom is sure to delight younger guests.

The modern and fully equipped kitchen allows you to indulge in culinary creations while surrounded by stunning views of the natural landscape. Whether you're cooking up a gourmet meal or a quick snack, the kitchen is a place where culinary magic happens.

Unwind and recharge in the lap of luxury at Kereru Chalet. With its harmonious blend of nature and comfort, it's the perfect destination for those seeking a tranquil escape without compromising on modern amenities.
